Coloradans have felt first-hand the effects of the failed Obama administration &mdash; President Obama has overregulated, overspent and made job-growth more difficult. With the hard work of individuals like Leondray, we can bring positive change to Washington and Colorado."<p>"I have studied, reflected, and selected the champion I see most fit and able to be president in this time of national and international difficulty," said Leondray Gholston.
