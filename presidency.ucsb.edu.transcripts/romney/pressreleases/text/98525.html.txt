"Governor Romney is a strong leader with a proven track record of addressing the most pressing issues of our time - growing the economy and creating jobs for American workers. I am proud to give him my support, and I am confident he will be our next president."<p><b>Background on Senator Richard Burr:</b><p>Senator Richard Burr has represented North Carolina in the U.S. Senate since 2005. Before being elected to the Senate, Burr served five terms in the U.S. House of Representatives. He previously worked as a sales manager for Carswell Distributing Company.
